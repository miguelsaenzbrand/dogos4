About us

Dublin Dogos RFC is a new club formed by expats and Irish players with the aim to foster integration through rugby under the slogan “one club, all countries”.

The team functions as a “permanent Barbarian” team, combining talent from different nationalities to compete at the highest level and bring together the expats and rugby communities in Ireland.

The year 2017 saw the birth of the Dogos and also the following milestones:

  • Deal with Coolmine RFC. Dogos will train and play home matches at Coolmine RFC in Ashtown, one of the finest rugby pitches in Ireland.
  • Affiliation with DCU Rugby. Dogos will be the team for DCU students at a club level. DCU Force will give the Dogos the depth and strength necessary to compete at the highest level.
  • Dogos Opening ceremony. In the form of a Tag Rugby tournament, with +400 attendees and a massive Argentinian ‘asado’ and South African ‘braai’ (BBQ).
  • Leinster league registration. Dogos will compete with two teams in the Leinster Metropolitan League. The first team will compete in the Metro 3 Division and the second team will compete in Metro 11.
